The IDR Mission

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments.

We bridge the gap between academic insight through our UK university partnerships and deployment into real-world projects.

Service Pillars

Academy

Specialized training and bootcamps for students and professionals entering the risk landscape.

Innovation

Developing IP, future risk models, and robust AI governance frameworks.

03

Advisory

Assisting organizations with the implementation of NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2 frameworks.

Our Community

We serve students, early-career professionals, and practitioners in sectors like financial services and critical infrastructure seeking upskilling in digital and cyber risk.
